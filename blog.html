Yummy! Laksa noodle soup

10 Feb, 2021 Singapore

These is an idiom in China, we say 民以食为天 (min yi shi wei tian)，it means Foods are the most important to the people. It comes from a historical record of Han dynasty: People are the most important to an emperor, while foods are the most important to the people .

For chinese folks, noodles and rice are the most important food. Since I moved to Singapore, I find this curry and coconut milk based soup with noodle is extrelly fit my tongue, that's Singapore Laska!

Have you tried Laksa? If you haven't, don't worry, I'll show you more details, and then you will know what's inside and how it might taste.

If you like spicy food and haven't tried Laksa, I highly recommend you to try it out. You will then never forget the special taste. Laksa is one of the most popular cuisines in Southeast Asia. You can find Laksa in alomst every Hawker center in Singapore.

So what is Laksa? According to Wikipedia Laksa is a spicy noodle soup popular in the Peranakan cuisine of Southeast Asia. Laksa consists of thick wheat noodles or rice vermicelli with chicken, prawn or fish, served in spicy soup based on either rich and spicy curry coconut milk or on sour asam(tamarind or gelugur). Laksa is found in Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore(where it has its own Katong laksa variant), and southen Thailand.
