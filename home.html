Yummy! Laksa noodle soup

These is an idiom in China, we say "民以食为天" (min yi shi wei tian)，it means "Foods are the most important to the people". It comes from a historical record of Han dynasty: People are the most important to an emperor, while foods are the most important to the people .

For me, noodles and rice are the most important food. Since I moved to Singapore, I find this curry and coconut milk based soup with noodle is extrelly fit my tongue, that's Singapore Laska !

Have you tried Singapore Laksa? If you haven't, don't worry, I'll show you more details, and then you will know what's inside and how it might taste.
